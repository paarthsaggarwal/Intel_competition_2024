Transpiration is a natural process that occurs in plants where they lose water from their leaves through tiny openings called stomata. Stomata are found on the surface of leaves and stems. During transpiration, water is pulled up from the roots of the plant and travels through the stem to the leaves. Once in the leaves, the water evaporates and escapes into the air as water vapor.
